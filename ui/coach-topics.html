CFOP

CFOP is by far the most popular for solving the 3x3. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced CFOP user, I can help you get faster with CFOP.

For the beginners, we'll focus on solving Cross and F2L efficiently. We will also teach you how to memorize the OLL and PLL algorithms without too much hassle.

For the advanced cubers, we'll take a more personalized approach. We will analyze your solves and tackle your weaknesses head-on.

Perhaps you rotate too much during F2L, or maybe you need to work on AUF prediction. Whatever the case may be, we have the knowledge and experience to help.

Roux

Roux is considered a rebel among the 3x3 methods. It's not the most popular, but it has a lot of fans.

Personally for me, Roux is my main method and the method I have used in my entire cubing career. I can teach you everything: how to blockbuild the first block efficiently, how to solve the second block quickly, how to learn all of the CMLL algorithms, and advanced method for the last six edges (LSE).

2x2

2x2 is a very fun event to learn and master.

If you are a beginner, we will focus on the Ortega method since it requires very few algorithms. If you are more ambitious with your 2x2 goals, we will help you learn CLL, EG1, and EG2. In total, that are over a hundred algorithms to learn, but we will teach you special memorization techniques.

In addition to algorithms, another important element of 2x2 is one-looking. One-looking is the ability to paln the entire solve during inspection. We will help you learn how to one-look and become a 2x2 master.

Turning speed

Turning fast is an important part of solving fast.

To help you turn faster, we will teach you the best fingertricks and ensure that your cube is set up properly to allow for fast turning.
